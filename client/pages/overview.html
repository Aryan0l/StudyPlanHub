About The Project

Planora turns study goals into a shared, trackable routine.

This project is built to make learning plans easier to create, easier to follow, and easier to stick with over time. Instead of vague goals, learners get a clean daily structure with visible progress and community feedback.

Community-first planning Creators publish structured pathways that other learners can follow and rate.
Progress that stays visible Daily tasks, saved completion, and plan details all stay in one focused flow.
Create Build day-by-day study plans with clear task breakdowns.
Follow Save strong plans from the community and keep them close.
Track Mark completed tasks and keep momentum visible over time.

What this project does

A quick view of the core product experience inside Planora.

01

Structured plan creation

Users can publish study plans with a title, description, subject, duration, and day-wise tasks.

02

Community discovery

Learners can browse, search, filter, rate, and follow plans that look useful and trustworthy.

03

Personal progress tracking

Every followed plan can become an active routine with saved task completion and visible progress.

Why It Exists

Planora is meant to reduce friction between intention and daily execution.

A lot of learners know what they want to study, but not how to convert that into a steady routine. This project fills that gap with reusable plans, social proof, and a calmer path from idea to action.

Frontend

Designed for clarity

Backend
